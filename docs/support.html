FAQ

Does AXON require an internet connection?

No. AXON works entirely offline. All games, assessments, and features function without any network connection.

Does AXON collect my data?

No. AXON collects zero data. All your progress, scores, and cognitive profile are stored locally on your device. Nothing is sent anywhere. See our Privacy Policy for full details.

What happens if I delete the app?

All your data is permanently deleted. Since everything is stored locally, there is no way to recover it after uninstalling.

How does the adaptive difficulty work?

AXON features 5 difficulty tiers that adjust in real time based on your performance. As you answer correctly and build streaks, the difficulty increases. If you struggle, it eases back to keep you in your optimal challenge zone.

What is the Cognitive Assessment?

When you first use AXON, you complete a 3-minute baseline assessment that measures your performance across 7 cognitive domains. Weekly assessments then track how your abilities change over time.

Can I skip the weekly assessment?

Yes. Tap "Play Later" when the assessment prompt appears. You can play normally, though your XP multiplier will stay at 1.0x until you complete the assessment.

The app isn't working properly. What should I do?

Try force-closing and reopening the app. If the issue persists, please email us at support@axonbrain.app with a description of the problem and your device model.
